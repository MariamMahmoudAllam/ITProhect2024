ABOUT EL GOUNA

El Gouna is a popular resort town located on the Red Sea coast of Egypt. It's known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and picturesque landscape. Developed in the 1980s by Egyptian businessman Samih Sawiris, El Gouna is often referred to as the "Venice of the Red Sea" due to its network of scenic lagoons and canals.To know more , you can click here
